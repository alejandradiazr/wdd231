Phone Center Site Plan

Site Name

Phone Center

This name was selected because it clearly represents the business, which specializes in smartphones, accessories, technical support, and repair services.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about available smartphones, accessories, repair services, promotions, and store contact information. Visitors will be able to browse products and contact the business through an online form.

Scenarios

  • What iPhone models are currently available at Phone Center?
  • How can I request a repair service for my phone?
  • What accessories are available for my smartphone?

Color Schema

  • Blue (#2D7FF9): Used for headers, navigation, buttons, and headings.
  • Light Gray (#F5F7FA): Used for page backgrounds and content sections.
  • Orange (#FFB347): Used for highlights, accents, and call-to-action buttons.

Typography

  • Poppins: Used for headings and navigation.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text and general content.
